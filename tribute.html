H.E. Emilio Mwai Kibaki

(1931 – 2022) | Third President of the Republic of Kenya

A Visionary Leader

Mwai Kibaki was an acclaimed economist and technocrat whose presidency reshaped Kenya’s economic and educational landscape. His leadership rescued the nation from stagnation and set it on a path of growth marked by fiscal discipline and development.

Defining Pillars of His Legacy

1. Free Primary Education (FPE)

In 2003, Kibaki abolished fees in public primary schools, allowing over one million new learners to enroll.

2. Economic Revival

His administration lifted GDP growth from under 1% to over 7% before the 2007 crisis.

3. Infrastructure Revolution

He championed major projects like the Thika Superhighway and national road improvements.

4. Decentralization & Higher Education

He introduced the CDF and expanded public universities from six to over twenty.
